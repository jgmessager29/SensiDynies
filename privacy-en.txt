PRIVACY POLICY (EN) - SENSIDYNIES BOT
English version

● Data Collected
The bot collects no personal data.
Only temporary technical information provided automatically by Discord is processed.

● Purpose of Information
Temporary data is used only for:
▸ executing commands
▸ applying moderation systems
▸ providing automated functions
No marketing analysis or profiling is performed.

● Data Sharing
The bot does not transmit any data to third parties.
No information is exported outside of Discord.

● Storage
The bot performs no persistent storage.
It uses:
▸ no external database
▸ no message retention system
▸ no permanent logs of users

● Security
Technical measures ensure safe handling of the temporary information required for the bot’s operation.

● Use on Third-Party Servers
This Policy applies equally on any server where the bot is installed.

● Modifications
The Owner may update this Policy at any time.
Continued use of the bot indicates acceptance of the updated terms.

● Contact
